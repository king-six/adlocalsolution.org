WHO WE ARE

We are a local firm bringing together indivIduals with diverse expertises and over 30 years of combined experiences working with multilateral donors and International organizations providing programmatic and operational support.
Our results are produced by the complementarity of the expertise of our inhouse teams, partners and the network of experts we have available to support our clients.

We are a multilingual interlocutor predisposed to participate fully in the development effort of West Africa.

Our Values and Principles

Excellence




The cult of excellence is an obsession for us. It translates into a permanent desire to achieve the expected results and satisfaction of the partners.


Pragmatism




Beyond the adage "is true only what really works", the partners share the same
desire to be simple, fast and properly.


Pedagogic

Pedagogy is one of the founding pillars of our companies. How to tackle complex issues in a simple and effective way? How to take into account the context and needs of all actors? How to drive change through appropriate pedagogy? It is to all these questions that we work to answer in our missions of support and collaboration.

Humanism


We are as committed to the human and professional values of our clients, beneficiaries, and employees. This humanistic dimension in our principles of operation and development contributes to the success of the firm's missions.
